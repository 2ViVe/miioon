Can I return or exchange goods I have received?

For the Ruckjack:

If you don’t like your design you can return it at your own cost within 30 days and we will provide you with a credit note or happily exchange it for another.

T-shirt and Swoopercape orders:

Please remember that every order you receive from Miioon is a unique, and custom made item that has been especially made to your child’s needs. This is what distinguishes us from more traditional clothing manufacturers. Please note returned goods cannot be resold and are either donated or thrown away.

If you do make a mistake with your order, call us and we will see what we can reasonably do to help you get the garment you want.

However, if the print or garment is faulty please email us TheOons@Miioon.com with a photograph (no need to send back) and we will happily send you a new one.

What can you do to help us keep our promise?

  • Please use our top tips for measuring.
  • Check your order’s email confirmation immediately.
  • Order as soon as possible if your order is designed for a specific date.

We are fast, but we cannot guarantee that your order will arrive at your door the very next day. Sometimes there can be delays due to design problems or the product not being in stock. We cannot accept a product being a day late for a birthday as a reason for return.

Delivery times

Your items are printed by hand and dispatched no later than 2 days after your order. Please note that we have no impact on the actual delivery time, and that the calculation of delivery times are only estimates. We have no influence on delivery times.

Production 1-2 business days + delivery = 5-7 business days.
