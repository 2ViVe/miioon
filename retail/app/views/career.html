Tips and FAQs

How do I sell?

This is the fun part - get creative! You are the boss of your business!!
But here are just a few ideas to get you started:

  • ▪ Launch your business and invite friends and family to a miioon party! Let children be your models and have fun introducing guests to the world of miioon.
  • ▪ miioon gift cards and technology make repeat sales easy.
  • Take your pop-up shop to fairs, charity events, craft markets, Christmas fetes, mother & toddler groups, school fairs, church bazaars.
  • ▪ Leave your gift card stands where people shop locally, and where an easy to give miioon gift card could save your customer time (coffee shops, hair salons, your neighbourhood shops).
  • ▪ Leave your miioon cards where people work, play, and wait!
  • ▪ Hook up with other social selling mums (gadgets, jewellery) to provide a 'market place' that covers everyone's gift giving needs.
  • ▪ Sell to friends and mummy networks through Facebook and Twitter.
