Digital Direct Printing

  • Digital Direct works well with large designs, the colors will fade over time for that desired "vintage" look.
  • We believe (after rigorous testing) that digital is the best quality look on light colours.

Digital Transfer Printing

  • Creating a true picture image as if you were there.

Wash me when I whiff

Digital designs can be preserved very well for 1-2 years if the recommended care instructions are followed.

  • T-shirts should be washed inside out and set on a gentle machine cycle in cold water at 30 degrees.
  • To avoid any form of fading, drying is not recommended.
  • Lay flat to dry.
  • Dry cleaning is not recommended.
